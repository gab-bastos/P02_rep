The bigger headings, H1 and H2 are in Pier Sans Bold while the smaller headings and paragraphs are in Lato. H1-H3 are also in upper case with more letter spacing to further differentiate the hierarchy. The colors are also different, H1 is the lightest and paragraphs are the darkest (except h4), to make ensure legibility.
