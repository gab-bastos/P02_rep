About The Founder

After moving to Canada with his family, Gabriel fell in love with playing guitar at his High School music class. He bought his first used guitar in 2016, a black Squier Bullet Strat, the classic beginner guitar. That guitar inspired him to practice daily, leading to rapid improvement. After about a year or so, he bought his first semi-professional level guitar, a PRS SE Zach Meyers Signature, to be able to play at events and church services.

Since then, Gabriel's expanded on his collection rapidly, with each guitar offering a different aspect to playing music, a different flavour. His belief, and part of his reason to start Strings and Things, is that although purchasing a guitar might not necessarily make you a better player, but it can certainly inspire you in a different way, pushing you to practice and improve your creativity as a musician.
